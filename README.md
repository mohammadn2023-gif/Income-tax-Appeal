# Income Tax Appeal Draft (Anonymized)
This repository contains a sample anonymized income tax appeal draft prepared for educational purposes.
## Key Features:
- Structured Statement of Facts
- Detailed Grounds of Appeal
- Legal reasoning and argument building
- Based on real-world tax litigation scenarios
## Use Case:
I use AI tools to assist in drafting:
- Income tax assessment replies
- GST notices and appeals
- Legal representations
## Objective:
To explore the use of AI in legal-tech, tax automation, and document drafting.
## Disclaimer:
All personal details have been anonymized.
